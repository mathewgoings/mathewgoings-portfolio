Snapshot TOPS HR Automation

Overview

Enterprise program to automate CARE onboarding, standardize conversions (Loyalty, VR, Tech), implement reporting governance, reduce roster work, and enable regional rollouts (Puerto Rico).

What I Built

  • Workday → TOPS workflows triggered by welcome-letter signatures, with delegate/I-9 rules to avoid locked I-9s.
  • Conversion variants for Loyalty, VR, and Tech with job-code mapping and governance checkpoints.
  • Reporting governance separating BOG (escalation) and Weekly Progress (coaching), with standard cadence (Fri → Tue 9am).
  • Roster automation via Power Automate & SharePoint integration, removing manual roster creation.

Role & Contributions

Architecture, requirements, stakeholder alignment, SOP documentation, partner with IT/HR for build, trainer enablement, and rollout support.

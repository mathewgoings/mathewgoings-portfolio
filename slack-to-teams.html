Slack to Teams Operational Stabilization

T-Mobile Enterprise Communication Recovery Initiative
Professional Profile T-Mobile: TOPS Project T-Mobile: HR Automation T-Mobile: Slack → Teams

Project Overview

During T-Mobile’s transition from Slack to Microsoft Teams, structured communication workflows were disrupted across multiple CEC sites. Within 48 hours, KPIs declined due to missed or delayed expert support requests.

Recognizing the breakdown early, I designed and deployed a structured Microsoft Teams Approvals workflow to restore communication clarity, accountability, and KPI stability.

Problem Identified

  • Slack workflows previously ensured structured help routing.
  • Teams chat lacked visibility and accountability tracking.
  • Expert requests were lost in chat threads.
  • Immediate KPI degradation observed across sites.

Action Taken

  • Rapid Skill Acquisition: Self-trained in Microsoft Teams and Office ecosystem tools to identify alternative workflow solutions.
  • Workflow Design: Built structured Approvals process to route requests, assign accountability, and track response status.
  • Pilot Program: Tested with three Rochester team managers for one week.
  • Site Deployment: Partnered with Senior Managers to roll out across all Rochester teams.
  • Multi-Site Rollout: Assisted admins from each CEC with setup and follow-ups at 1- and 2-week checkpoints.

Results

  • Rochester KPIs stabilized within one week.
  • All sites returned to pre-transition KPI levels by Week 2.
  • Communication clarity restored.
  • Help requests no longer missed in Teams chat.
  • Enterprise accountability framework established.

Capabilities Demonstrated

  • Operational risk detection
  • Rapid solution design under pressure
  • Microsoft Teams Approvals deployment
  • KPI stabilization strategy
  • Cross-site stakeholder coordination
  • Change management execution
